Nurse Assistant Training Test Bank


Welcome to the American Red Cross Nurse Assistant Training Test Bank. The Test Bank is a ready-made electronic testing resource of more than 500 questions that can be customized by instructors for the teaching of the Nurse Assistant Training course and for assessing student progress.

There is just one file to download and it consists of the Test Bank and the TestGen software. When you download the Testing Program, the Test Bank will already be in the TestGen Software library. The Test Bank is not usable without the TestGen program.

When software updates become available, you will receive automatic notification when you sign into TestGen and connect to the Internet.
