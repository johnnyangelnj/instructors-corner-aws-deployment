Scientific Advisory Council

Description: In late 1998, the Red Cross formed an independent panel of nationally recognized health and safety experts known as the Scientific Advisory Council. Drawing on a body of collective expertise from such diverse fields as emergency medicine, occupational health, sports medicine, school health, emergency medical services (EMS) response and disaster mobilization, The Council helps to establish the standard in first aid care. It also advises the Red Cross in areas related to the development and dissemination of audience-appropriate information and training in first aid and safety.
